FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about using Nomoads as your mobile ad blocker on Android and iOS.

Is Nomoads an ad blocker app for Android or just a browser extension?

Nomoads is a system-wide DNS-based ad blocker app for Android, not a browser extension. It works across all apps and browsers on your device, not just inside a single browser.

Will a DNS ad blocker slow down my phone or battery?

Nomoads uses an optimized, on-device DNS engine designed for minimal CPU and battery impact. In many cases, blocking ads and trackers actually makes pages load faster.

Does Nomoads see or log my browsing history?

No. All DNS filtering happens locally on your device and your traffic is never proxied through external VPN servers. We don't collect, log, or sell your browsing data.

Can I whitelist apps or websites that break with an ad blocker?

Yes. Nomoads lets you exclude specific apps from protection and add domains to an allowlist so you can support sites you trust or fix compatibility issues.

Does Nomoads work on iOS?

Yes. Nomoads is on the App Store for iPhone and iPad, with the same privacy-first, on-device DNS filtering as on Android.

What's the difference between the free and Premium plans?

The free Basic plan includes core system-wide ad blocking and essential blocklists. Premium unlocks extra protection levels, advanced blocklists, and more customization for power users.
